Growth Summary & Projection:
The Instagram account showed clear momentum across the funnel: strong increases in views and especially reach, followed by meaningful growth in interactions, profile visits and link clicks. This indicates that the campaign is not only generating visibility, but also driving action and intent.

Projection: If we keep the same creative consistency and maintain optimization, we expect continued growth in reach and engagement, with a higher volume of profile actions converting into leads. The fastest path to scale is to (1) test new creatives per audience (athletes, seniors, pain relief), (2) strengthen the CTA and form flow to improve click-to-lead conversion, and (3) remarket to visitors and clickers to increase lead capture without raising costs proportionally.
